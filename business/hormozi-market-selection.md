# Hormozi Market Selection Prompts

> Source: Acquisition.com AI (Alex Hormozi)
> Framework: $100M Offers — Health, Wealth, or Relationships
> Use these prompts to pick a target market with AI assistance.

---

## Prompt 1: The "4 Market Filters" Deep Dive

```
Help me pick a market using your 4 criteria:
1. Massive pain
2. Purchasing power
3. Easy to target
4. Growing

I'm starting from zero. Ask me whatever you need about my skills, experience, and interests, then:
- Propose 3 concrete markets in Health, Wealth, or Relationships
- Score each 1–10 on pain, purchasing power, ease of targeting, and growth
- Tell me which ONE you'd choose and why, assuming I just want the best odds of success, not "passion."

Use your "starving crowd" and market criteria frameworks.
```

**References:** $100M Offers pp. 37–41 · $100M Playbook Branding p. 23

---

## Prompt 2: Use My Past To Choose My Richest Market

```
I'm starting a business from scratch. I'll list:
- My past jobs
- Skills I'm at least above average at
- Groups of people I understand well (demographics / professions / hobbies)

Using that, pick ONE best market for me in Health, Wealth, or Relationships that:
- Has big pain
- Has money
- Is easy to find online or offline
- Is growing, not shrinking

Explain your choice using your market selection rules and why the others lose.
```

**References:** $100M Offers pp. 38–41

---

## Prompt 3: Rich vs Poor Market Filter

```
I want to avoid poor markets (like unemployed people or dying industries) and pick a "rich" market.
I'll tell you who I think I want to help. Your job:
- Diagnose if that market fails any of your 4 checks (pain, purchasing power, easy to target, growing)
- If it fails, suggest a richer adjacent market that keeps the same type of problem but upgrades the buyer
  (e.g., from unemployed to employed, from dying industry to growing one)

Be ruthless and specific. Use your resume-writer and newspaper examples to explain.
```

**References:** $100M Offers pp. 38–40

---

## Prompt 4: Niche Me Down So I Can Charge 10–100x

```
I'll describe a broad area I'm interested in (like "fitness," "making money," "dating," etc.).
Using your "riches are in the niches" approach, help me:
- Place it under Health, Wealth, or Relationships
- Niche it down to a very specific avatar + problem so that the same core solution could sell for 10–100x more
- Make sure that avatar passes your 4 market tests

Then tell me the exact final market you'd have me serve.
```

**References:** $100M Offers pp. 41, 45 · $100M Playbook Branding p. 23

---

## Prompt 5: Pick My Market Like I Want $1M, Not A Hobby

```
Assume I don't care about passion, only about building a $1M+ business as fast as realistically possible,
and I'm willing to do boring work for years.

Ask me 5 questions max about:
- My skills and experience
- Time I can commit weekly
- Whether I prefer online / offline work

Then:
- Recommend ONE market
- Prove it meets your 4 indicators
- Briefly outline the first offer you'd build for that market so I can see why it's attractive
```

**References:** $100M Offers pp. 38–40 · $100M Playbook Branding p. 23

## Quick Reference: Hormozi's 4 Market Indicators

| Indicator | Question to Ask |
|---|---|
| Massive Pain | Are they desperate for a solution? |
| Purchasing Power | Can they actually pay for it? |
| Easy to Target | Can you find them online or offline? |
| Growing | Is the market expanding, not dying? |

**The three macro markets:** Health · Wealth · Relationships
