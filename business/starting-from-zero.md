# Hormozi AI — 5 Starting-From-Zero Prompts

> Source: Acquisition.com AI (Alex Hormozi)  
> Use: Copy-paste the prompt you need, fill in the brackets, and submit.

---

## 1. Offer & Avatar Clarity

**Goal:** Define your best avatar, their pains/outcomes, and a Grand Slam Offer with a 90-day money model.

```
Act as my offer architect. I'm starting from scratch in [your niche/skill set]. Ask me the minimum questions needed, then:

- Define my single best starting avatar
- Describe their top 3 pains and dream outcomes
- Propose ONE Grand Slam Offer with price, deliverables, guarantee, and bonuses
- Explain how this offer fits into a simple money model for the first 90 days.
```

---

## 2. First 10 Customers Plan (Rule of 100)

**Goal:** Get your first 10 customers in 14 days using only free methods and one primary channel.

```
I have no audience and no customers. Based on this offer: [paste offer], design a 14-day 'First 10 Customers' plan using only free methods.

- Choose ONE primary channel (DMs, cold email, calls, content, or networking)
- Give me a daily checklist using the Rule of 100 (100 minutes or 100 outreaches)
- Include exact outreach scripts and how to track the numbers in a simple sheet.
```

---

## 3. Minimal Tech, Max Cash Setup

**Goal:** Design the simplest operating system to sell and deliver your offer with limited budget and tools.

```
I'm starting with $[X] and basic tools only. Design the simplest possible operating system to sell and deliver my offer:

- What platforms to use for calendar, payments, and communication
- The exact steps from stranger → lead → booked call → paid → fulfilled
- The 3 numbers I must track daily and the targets for the first 30 days.
```

---

## 4. Sales Call Script & Objection Handling

**Goal:** A complete 1-call close script with objection responses you can memorize and role-play.

```
Using this offer and avatar: [paste], write a complete 1-call close script:

- Opening, agenda, and discovery questions
- Transition into pitch framed around your value equation
- Word-for-word responses to the 5 most likely objections
- A simple 'yes or no' close I can memorize and role-play.
```

---

## 5. Weekly Execution Cadence (Founder OS)

**Goal:** An 8-week solo founder schedule that maximizes momentum with your available hours.

```
I'm a solo founder starting from scratch with [hours] per week available. Build me a weekly schedule that maximizes momentum for the next 8 weeks:

- Exact time blocks for offer work, lead gen, sales, and fulfillment
- What I should review every Sunday (numbers + decisions)
- A simple rule set for when to raise prices, when to change the offer, and when to add a new channel.
```

## How to Use

Pick one prompt, fill in all `[brackets]`, paste it into the Acquisition.com AI, and get a wired system back.

**Suggested starting order if you're at zero:**

1. Prompt 1 — nail your offer first
2. Prompt 3 — set up the tech/ops
3. Prompt 2 — go get customers
4. Prompt 4 — close them
